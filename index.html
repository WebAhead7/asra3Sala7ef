AFFORDABLE & TRUSTED VOLUNTEER ABROAD PROGRAMS:
Asra3 sala7ef is an award-winning volunteer travel organisation, offering life-changing experiences across 15 countries in Asia, Europe & South America. PMGY provide a safe, structured and social platform with volunteer travel opportunities available from 1-40 weeks. With over 10 years of experience and expertise, Asra3 sala7ef is widely regarded as the global leader in affordable and trustworthy international volunteering experiences..

We work hard to ensure that our volunteering opportunities are truly affordable. We are 100% transparent with our fees – something our volunteers really appreciate. Our structured programs create the perfect platform for you to see the world, meet like-minded people and engage in meaningful volunteering work. We have teams throughout our local destinations who will support you 24/7.

Furthermore, every program is regularly risk-assessed by our management team to ensure our set up is safe and that our volunteers are making a positive impact in the community.

There is also a great social element to our opportunities. Even if you’re travelling alone, you’re going to meet heaps of people along the way and make friends for life. Plus you’ll have plenty of opportunity to travel the country with your newfound travel buddies – it’s all part of the experience!
